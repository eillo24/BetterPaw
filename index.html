About Us

We know that rescuing a pet is an amazing thing, but also a heavy one. Between socializing and supporting your new buddy, on top of finding time to train them, it can be overwhelming. That’s where BetterPaw comes in.

We offer all the techniques and coaching that traditional puppy schools do, without the rigid schedule. BetterPaw strives to make it easier for dog owners to get that training time in. Boosting a loving community of more than 200+ users we make sure you and your dog feel supported throughout your journey. We take care of your time so you can make time.

Our Vision

In a world where feeling out of control is just another day. We believe that there is no shame, but strength and pride in asking for help when everything gets too much. And with a bit of extra love and patience, we can overcome our struggles in time. Only then can we get control to face the world head-on and enjoy the time we spend together.

Our Values

Love

One of the essential ingredients that you need to make everything come together. The love we give out and the love we receive from those around us helps us face a new date to make a better day.

Patience

The other essential ingredient to make our package complete. We at BetterPaw know that just having a little more patience when others need us to can go a long way to take back control we might have lost along the way.

Growth

Asking for help when you need it, is nothing to be ashamed of. BetterPaw strives to give you the help you need when you need it. Because asking for help is something to be proud of, and we provide.

Community

Community: Together we stand strong and the connections we make throughout our journeys to figure out our struggles give us the knowledge to help others and become closer.
